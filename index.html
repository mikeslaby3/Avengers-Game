Avengers Infinity Stone Challenge

Help the Avengers defeat Thanos!

In order to defeat Thanos and return the Infinity stones back into to the right hands, we must use all the heros left to attack him from all angles.


The four remaining Avengers have the capability of inflicting different amounts of damage. The only way to reclaim each stone, is to inflict the exact amount of damage as Thanos has health.


If you do so, you get one stone back. His health will then reset to a new number. If you inflict too much damage, he'll use the Time Stone to go back to full health. Be careful, if this happens 10 times, he'll have the power to wipe out the remaining Avengers.


Once we reclaim all five stones, Thanos will finally be defeated. As the commander, it's YOUR responsibility to tell each Avenger when to attack.
